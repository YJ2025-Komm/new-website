# GeoRankers: AI Search Intelligence Platform

## About GeoRankers

GeoRankers is a B2B SaaS platform that helps companies optimize their visibility in AI-powered search results. As artificial intelligence models like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity become primary sources of information discovery, traditional SEO strategies are insufficient.

## What We Do

**Core Mission**: Help B2B SaaS companies track, optimize, and build the brand authority needed to get visible in AI-generated search results and recommendations.

**Primary Problems Solved**:
1. **Brand Invisibility in AI Discovery** - Many companies are completely invisible when users ask AI models for product recommendations or industry solutions
2. **Content Strategy Guesswork** - Businesses lack data-driven insights about what content will perform well in AI search
3. **Competitive Disadvantage** - Companies are losing market share to competitors who appear in AI-generated recommendations

## Key Features & Solutions

### 1. Predictive AI Search Forecasting
- Analyzes historical AI search data to forecast emerging topics and questions
- Helps companies build forward-looking content strategies
- Identifies trending queries before competitors discover them

### 2. Real-time AI Mention Monitoring  
- Tracks brand mentions across major AI platforms (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude)
- Monitors competitor visibility in AI search results
- Provides alerts when brand authority changes

### 3. Competitive AI Intelligence
- Analyzes which competitors appear in AI recommendations
- Identifies content gaps and opportunities
- Benchmarks brand performance against industry leaders

### 4. Content Strategy Optimization
- Data-driven recommendations for AI search visibility
- Content optimization suggestions based on AI model preferences
- Strategic guidance for building topical authority

## Industry Context

The rise of AI-powered search represents a fundamental shift in how people discover information and make purchasing decisions. Traditional search engine optimization (SEO) focused on Google's algorithms, but AI search optimization requires understanding how large language models surface and recommend content.

**Key Trend**: Users increasingly ask AI models questions like "What's the best CRM for small businesses?" or "Which marketing automation tools should I consider?" Companies that appear in these AI-generated responses gain significant competitive advantages.
