1. Knowledge & Authority (0–20 pts)
Measures structured presence + organic authority.
Q1. Wikipedia / Knowledge Graph
Does your brand have a dedicated Wikipedia page or appear in Google Knowledge Graph (right-hand panel in Google search)?
	• Yes, both = 10 pts
	• Partial (either Wiki or KG) = 5 pts
	• None = 0 pts
Q2. Profiles on Crunchbase / G2 / Capterra
Is your brand listed on Crunchbase, G2, or Capterra?
	• Yes, on 2+ platforms = 5 pts
	• Yes, on 1 platform = 2 pts
	• None = 0 pts
Q3. Google Page-1 Visibility
How many of your website’s pages currently rank on Page 1 of Google for your target keywords?
	• 10+ pages = 5 pts
	• 1–9 pages = 3 pts
	• None = 0 pts
👉 Total possible: 20 pts

2. Community Signals (0–20 pts)
Captures real-world chatter + peer validation.
Q4. Reddit Mentions
When you search your brand on Reddit, do you find active discussions or mentions?
	• Yes, multiple threads with engagement = 7 pts
	• A few mentions, low activity = 3 pts
	• None = 0 pts
Q5. Reddit Karma
Approximate total upvotes/karma across brand mentions.
	• 100+ karma = 7 pts
	• 1–99 karma = 3 pts
	• 0 = 0 pts
Q6. LinkedIn / Product Hunt Mentions
When you search your brand on LinkedIn or Product Hunt, do you see posts/discussions?
	• Yes, user-driven discussions = 6 pts
	• Only brand-led posts = 3 pts
	• None = 0 pts
👉 Total possible: 20 pts
