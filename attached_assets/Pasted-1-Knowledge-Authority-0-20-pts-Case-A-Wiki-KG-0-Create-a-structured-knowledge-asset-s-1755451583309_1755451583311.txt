3. Reviews & Reputation (0–20 pts)

👉 “Set up profiles on at least 2 review sites where your buyers search. G2 + Capterra are table stakes for B2B.”

👉 “Start replying to every review — even negative ones. LLMs pick up on signals of responsiveness and credibility.”

Case C: Only one platform
👉 “Expand beyond a single platform. If you’re on G2, add Capterra or Trustpilot. More review surfaces = more AI visibility.”

4. Media & Coverage (0–20 pts)

Case B: Only niche blogs

Case C: Tier-1 present but inconsistent
