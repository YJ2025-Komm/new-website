**GeoRankers: Own Your Presence in AI Search**

GeoRankers is a visibility intelligence tool that helps brands understand how they show up in AI-generated search results across platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude.

Just as SEO tools helped brands rank on Google, GeoRankers helps brands track and improve their presence in AI search—the next major discovery channel.

As people increasingly ask LLMs for recommendations (e.g., "Best CRM for small businesses"), most brands have no idea whether they’re being mentioned, ignored, or misrepresented in those answers. GeoRankers solves that.

---

**Why It Matters**

* AI is replacing traditional keyword search with contextual, generative answers
* Users trust those AI answers—often without clicking to verify
* If your brand is missing in AI responses, you’re invisible in the new search paradigm

GeoRankers gives brands back their control and visibility—helping them:

* Know where they’re being surfaced
* See how competitors are doing
* Take action to improve their AI search presence

---

**Core Features**

**1. AI Search Presence Checker**
Enter your brand name or website to check if it appears in answers from:

* ChatGPT
* Gemini
* Perplexity
* Claude

Results display whether you are mentioned, and where.

**2. Prompt Engine (LLM Search Simulation)**
Simulates how real users ask AI by generating diverse prompts like:

* "What are the best customer engagement tools in 2025?"
* "Which AI platforms are best for small businesses?"

The engine feeds these prompts to LLMs and extracts brand mentions from responses.

**3. AI Visibility Score (Signal Tracker)**
A simple score built on:

* Number of AI tools that mention your brand
* Type of mention (top pick, listed, side reference)
* Quality of source referenced (G2, Reddit, YouTube, etc.)
* Frequency of appearance across different prompt variants

**4. Competitor Comparison**
Add your top competitors and instantly benchmark your AI visibility against theirs.

**5. Source Breakdown**
Uncover the sources AI tools are referencing when they mention your brand:

* G2, Capterra, Medium, Reddit, Product Hunt, YouTube, etc.
* Know where to double down with reviews, backlinks, or content.

**6. Actionable Recommendations**
Based on your visibility profile, GeoRankers recommends:

* Platforms to optimize (e.g., G2, Capterra)
* Types of content to create (e.g., product comparisons, customer stories)
* Suggestions to improve your influence on AI-generated answers

**7. Report Export + Alerts**

* Downloadable visibility reports
* Set alerts when your brand drops in visibility or when competitors spike
